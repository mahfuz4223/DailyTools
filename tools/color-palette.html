What are Color Harmonies?

Color harmonies are combinations of colors based on their positions on the color wheel. They create visually pleasing and balanced color schemes that work well together in design.

Complementary

Colors opposite each other on the color wheel

Analogous

Colors adjacent to each other on the color wheel

Triadic

Three colors equally spaced on the color wheel

Monochromatic

Different shades of the same color
